Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites save on your device while browsing. These files contain information that is used to improve your experience on the site.

What cookies do we use?

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session cookies to maintain your login
  • Basic site preference cookies
  • Security and authentication cookies

Analytics Cookies (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information in aggregate form and include:

  • Number of visitors and visits
  • Time spent on the site
  • Pages visited
  • Traffic source

Your Cookie Choices

You can choose which cookies to accept through our cookie banner. Your options are:

  • Accept All: You accept the use of all cookies
  • Reject: We will only use essential cookies

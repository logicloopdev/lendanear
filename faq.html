Frequently Asked Questions

Is my conversation private?

Yes, all conversations with our AI are completely private and confidential. We use advanced encryption to protect your data, and we never share your information with third parties.

How does the AI support work?

Our AI is trained to provide empathetic responses and support. It can help you explore your feelings, offer coping strategies, and provide information about mental health resources. However, it's not a replacement for professional mental health care.

Can I use the service anonymously?

Yes, you can use our service without providing any personal information. We only require an email address for account creation, which is kept strictly confidential.

What if I need immediate help?

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services (911) or the National Crisis Hotline (988) immediately. Our service is not designed for emergency situations.

Is there a limit to how long I can chat?

No, there are no time limits on our chat sessions. You can talk for as long as you need, 24/7.

How do I report an issue?

If you encounter any technical issues or have concerns about the service, please use our contact form or email us at logicloop.dev@gmail.com. We take all feedback seriously and strive to improve our service continuously.
